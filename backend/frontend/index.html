How it works

Three steps, that's it

We kept it as simple as possible.

1

Type your question

Ask anything health-related, in your own words.

2

AI reads and thinks

It searches a medical knowledge base to find the right context.

3

You get an answer

Clear, concise, and right there in the chat.

⚠️

Just so you know

This is a prototype - not a medical service. It's meant to help you understand things, not replace a real doctor. Please consult a healthcare professional for anything serious. In an emergency, call your local emergency number.

About

Built for my final year project

I wanted to explore whether AI could actually be useful in healthcare - not in a scary, replace-your-doctor way, but in a simple "help me understand what's happening" way.

Medibot uses a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system built on top of a Groq-powered LLM, wrapped in a FastAPI backend with a straightforward frontend.

Python · FastAPI LangChain · RAG Groq LLM MongoDB Student project
